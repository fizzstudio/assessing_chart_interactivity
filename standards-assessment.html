Fizz Studio: Assessing Chart Accessibility and Usability

This small test environment is intended to be used to help evaluate access barriers in online, interactive charts and graphs. We are evaluating a series of interactive charts and graphs with a standards- and research-based assessment, Chartability. The specific criteria used in this evaluation may be found here.

In order to ensure fairness in evaluation, all assessments made are done by a pair: a primary analyst and then through a second opinion by another analyst. Consensus is reached when determining the outcome of the evaluation results. In addition, some tests were performed by experts in a given assistive technology.
